Command syntax is the following:

add square x1 y1 x2 y2 #color          (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) are opposite corners
add circle x1 y1 radius #color         (x1, y1) is the centre
add triangle x1 y1 x2 y2 x3 y3 #color  3 points representing vertices
translate x1 y1 x2 y2                  translate objects in (x1, y1) into (x2, y2)
scale sx sy x1 y1                      scale by factor s all objects in (x1, x2)
rotate angle x1 y1                     rotate angle degrees all objects in (x1, x2)
grid x_replicas y_replicas x1 y1       replicates the objects in (x1, y1)
undo                                   undoes the last command
redo                                   redoes the last undo command
